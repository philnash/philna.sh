In the last week, I announced that I'd built a time zone helper.
I also discovered Aurora Scharff's ClickLight and Fayaz Ahmed's ScreenDrop.

AI has been talked about as bringing on personal software, but as developers we have the ability to build small tools that will help more than just ourselves. Perhaps you vibe code a quick version for yourself, but when you find yourself using it over and over it means it could be good for others too, and we have the skills to move it from a vibe-coded personal piece of software to a mini app that can benefit others.

This reminds me of John Allsopp's [Disenshittify Project](https://deshittify.us/) from a couple of years ago. I don't think it took off much at the time, but since the ability to build small apps quickly has accelerated and keeps going.

https://www.tinytooltown.com/

As developers we can bring apps to life and give ourselves and users a better experience. Try using some of your tokens to build something small and disenshittify the web a bit more.
